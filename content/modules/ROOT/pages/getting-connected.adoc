= Getting Connected

In this activity, you will connect to the OpenShift console and verify your environment is ready for the workshop.

== Login to OpenShift

. Open the OpenShift console in your browser:
+
{openshift_console_url}

. Log in with the following credentials:
+
**Username:** `{user}`
+
**Password:** `{password}`

. Once logged in, you should see the OpenShift console Projects page.

== Verify the RHOAI dashboard

. In the top navigation bar, click the grid icon (application launcher).

. In the dropdown, under **OpenShift Self Managed Services**, click **Red Hat OpenShift AI**.
+
The RHOAI dashboard opens in a new browser tab. It may take a few seconds to load.

. Verify you see the RHOAI home page with the "Start by creating your project" message.

== End of activity

You are now logged in and ready to begin the workshop modules.
